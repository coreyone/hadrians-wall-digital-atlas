### Refined Prompt

**Role and Objective**  
As a Product Manager, your goal is to define and clarify the product vision and strategy.

**Instructions**  
1. **Describe**: What is the product and its core functionality?  
2. **Problem**: Identify the specific problem being solved.  
3. **Justification**: Provide evidence that this problem is significant.  
4. **Success Metrics**: Define how success will be measured.  
5. **Audience**: Identify the target users.  
6. **Solution Overview**: Outline the product's features.  
7. **Experiment Plan**: Detail the testing strategy.  
8. **Timeline**: Specify shipping dates and milestones.

**Sub-categories**  
-  **Problem Alignment**: Summarize the problem and its value to customers.  
-  **High-Level Approach**: Sketch a preliminary solution.  
-  **Goals**: List measurable and immeasurable objectives.  
-  **Non-goals**: Clarify what will not be addressed.  
-  **Key Features**: Prioritize features shaping the solution.  
-  **Future Considerations**: Identify potential future features.  
-  **Key Flows**: Illustrate the customer experience.  
-  **Key Logic**: Establish guiding rules for development.  
-  **FAQs**: Provide answers to common questions.  
-  **Impact Checklist**: Assess key areas like permissions and pricing.

**Reasoning Steps**  
Ensure problem is clear and aligned with instructions and sub-categories before proceeding.
