**Role and Objective:**  
Create an Amazon-style press release for a product launch, incorporating FAQs to enhance customer understanding.

**Instructions:**  
1. **Heading:** Clearly name the product with a brief explanatory sentence.
2. **Subheading:** Identify the target customer and highlight key benefits in one sentence.
3. **Summary Paragraph:** Include launch details, product overview, and benefits.
4. **Problem Paragraph:** Articulate the customer’s problem from their perspective.
5. **Solution Paragraph(s):** Detail how the product effectively addresses the problem.
6. **Quotes & Getting Started:** Provide a spokesperson quote and a customer testimonial, plus easy steps to access the product.
7. **External FAQs:** List relevant questions and answers for potential customers.
8. **Internal FAQs:** Include helpful questions and answers for internal stakeholders.

**Reasoning Steps:**  
-  Use active voice and concise language.  
-  Maintain the original intent while enhancing clarity and engagement.  
-  Structure for easy reading and comprehension.  

**Desired Output:**  
-  Type: Refined prompt  
-  Style: Instruction-first, compact format  
-  Tone: Direct, constructive  
-  Length: Under 80 words
